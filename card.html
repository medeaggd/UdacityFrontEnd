Lipizzan Horse

All Lipizzaner horses are descended from six stallions.

  • Scientific Name: Equus caballus
  • Other Names: Lipizzaner, Karster
  • Average Height: 14.7 hands (60"/152cm)
  • Average Lifespan: 27 years

This horse breed was created in the late 16th century in Slovenia, in order to fulfill demand among the aristocracy for a powerful yet agile horse. They are known for their development through the Spanish Riding School, which involves rigorous training to show off their skills.
